Stop Captain Cheems

You are working for an intelligence agency that is fighting against Memes Federation.

After months of hardworking, you finally have discovered the identity of one of their undercovered captain.

Now, your mission is to reveal their messages.

For this mission, you know that:

  • They use emojis to encrypt their messages.
  • They use 💯 as a number scape code.
  • They also use a scape code for capital letters.
  • The emojis used for their encryption are the following ones:
    🐶🐱🐭🐹🐰🦊🐻🐼🐨🐯🦁🐮🐷🐽🐸🐵🙈🙉🙊🐒🐔🐧🐦🐤🐣🐥🦆🦅🦉🦇🐺🐗🐴🦄🐝🪱🐛🦋🐌🐞🐜🪰🐬🪲🦟🦗🕷🕸🦂🐢🐍🦎🦖🦕

Your team has been working on this for a while, and they have detected the following rules:

  • The first row always contains the emission date.
  • The second row always says: "Captain Cheems reports:"
  • The last line always says: "God and Godzi save the Meems."

Additionally, your team has detected the idea behind their encryption process.

  • They choose an arbitrary emoji as the letter "a."
  • Then the letter "b" is the next emoji; If they have reached the last emoji and haven't covered all the letters, they will continue with the first emoji in the list.
  • Finally, the capital scape code is the emoji that follows the letter "z."
